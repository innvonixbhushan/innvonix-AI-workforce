Each AI operator is one stage of the Traffic & Revenue due-diligence process turned into an autonomous team of agents. They pull and clean ETC and IHMCL data, run the 10,000-plus survey hygiene checks, validate the base year, build the forecast with growth drivers, diversion and Monte Carlo scenarios, and assemble the NHAI-format report. Point one at a project or corridor and it runs the whole loop, then hands back an analysis-ready output for your analyst to validate before it goes to the client.
