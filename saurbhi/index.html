AI operators that find new stockists, grow range per store, win back lapsed orders, and plan the rep's week
Concept prototype  ·  illustrative
Each AI operator is a distribution sales or business-development workflow turned into an autonomous team of agents: they find new Indian grocery stores to sign, work out which of the 21 categories a store isn't buying yet, read the buyer-app order history to catch stores whose orders are slipping, build the trade quote, and plan the rep's week. Point one at a store or a territory and it runs the whole loop, then hands back an order-ready output for your sales manager to review.
Your sales operators
Stand up a new AI operator in three steps: pick the sales workflow, assign each task to an agent, then name it and set the cadence. It joins your sales team.
Concept prototype for Saurbhi / Shivom Enterprise (Australian distribution). Agents and outputs are illustrative simulations; store, order and price details are placeholders, not live trade data. A human sales manager reviews and approves every order, credit line and promotion.
