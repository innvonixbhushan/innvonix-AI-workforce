AI operators for the GPS product team and for CashPro clients: ISO 20022 readiness, onboarding, forecasting, exceptions and more
Independent concept  ·  illustrative
Each AI operator is a payments product or operations workflow turned into an autonomous team of agents. One track works for the GPS product and operations team: ISO 20022 readiness, client implementation, product requirements, RFP responses and regulatory intelligence. A second track is client-facing, extending CashPro: cash-flow forecasting, payment investigations and receivables reconciliation. Point one at a case or a client and it runs the whole loop, then hands back a review-ready output. Every output is reviewed and approved by a person, and anything client-facing would run inside the bank's own controls.
