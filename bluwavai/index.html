BluWave-ai · Agentic Energy Operations
AI operators that forecast, optimise and dispatch your renewable, storage and EV assets
Concept prototype  ·  illustrative
Each AI operator is an energy use case turned into an autonomous team of agents: forecasters, optimisers and validators that run the whole loop and return an operating decision. State an objective and the operator forecasts, optimises against price and constraints, and hands back a dispatch-ready plan with the cost and CO₂ impact.
Your operators on shift
Stand up a new AI operator in three steps: pick the use case, assign each task to an agent, then name it and set the cadence. It joins your operations team.
Concept prototype for BluWave-ai. Agents and outputs are illustrative simulations; figures are placeholders, not live optimisation results. Built to show the agentic-operations pattern on top of BluWave-ai predictors and optimisers.
